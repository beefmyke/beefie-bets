FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS BY OUR USERS

What does Home mean?

Home means that in the match, the Home team has been predicted to win the match. It can also be referred to as 1.

What does X mean?

X refers to a Tie or a draw in the match. It means that in the match, it has been predicted that there would be no winners nor losers. Example of a draw match is 1-1 or 0-0 (popularly known as eye-glass )

What does Away mean?

Away means that in the match, the Away team has been predicted to win the match. It can also be referred to as 2.

What does 1X mean?

1X means that it is either the home team wins or it draws the match. In other words it means that the home team has been predicted not to lose the match.

What does X2 mean?

X2 means that it is either the away or visiting team wins or draws the match. In otherwords it means that the away team has been predicted not to lose the match

What does GG mean?

GG stands for Goal Goal, also known as Both team to score. It means that, in a match, both teams have been predicted to score at least a goal each.

What does NG mean?

NG stands for NO GOAL GOAL. It simply means that only one team, either the home team or the away team has been predicted to score in the match.

What does Over 2.5 mean?

Over 2.5 stands for more than 2 goals in a match. It means that the total number of goals in a match shall surpass 2. In otherwords, the must be at least 3 goals scored in the match in consideration.

What does Under 2.5 mean?

Under 2.5 stands for less than 3 goals in a match. It means that the total number of goals in a match shall not surpass 2. In otherwords, there must be at least 2 goals scored in the match in consideration.

How do I identify the Home team in a match?

The home team, which is the host team(the team at whose stadium the match is to be played) is usually the first team in a match. for example in a match between FC Barcelona vs Chelsea FC, the home team could be seen as FC Barcelona.

How do I identify the Away team in a match?

The Away team, which is the visiting team(the travelling team) is usually the second team in a match. For example in a match between Real Madrid CF Vs Juventus FC, the away team could be seen as Juventus FC.

How do I make the best out of betting?

You should choose from the list of predictions which ones to bet on. Betting on every single match provided might reduce your chance of winning. This is usually the case regardless of how accurate the prediction might look or seem. Always remember the first Rule of gambling: GAMBLE THE AMOUNT YOU CAN AFFORD TO LOSE!. This means that you should endeavour to stake an amount that won't be detrimental to your finances should your bet fail.
